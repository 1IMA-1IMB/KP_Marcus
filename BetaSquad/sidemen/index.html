SIDEMEN Group

KSI is one of the founders and members of SIDEMEN, a group of British YouTube personalities collaborating to create diverse and entertaining content. Alongside six other members, including Miniminter, Zerkaa, TBJZL, Behzinga, Vikkstar123, and W2S, KSI plays a significant role in the SIDEMEN group.
